Weightlifting in the Gym

Starting to weightlift can be scary and confusing if you don't know where to start. For the longest time, I wanted to go to the gym and get stronger, but I felt intimidated and worried I would look stupid and not know what to do. After going to the gym with a friend for a while I became more confortable and now I am able to formulate my own work outs and I go to the gym by myself every day. I have created this page to help explain a basic workout split that anyone can do. Below I have attactched a youtube video that explains the type of workout split I like to do (Push, Pull Legs),



  1. Push (Chest, Triceps, Shoulders)
    • Barebell bench press
    • Tricep push down
    • Inclinde dumbbell bench press
    • dumbbell shoulder press
    • Dumbbell Lateral raises

  2. Pull (Back, Biceps)
    • Lateral pull downs
    • Seated cable rows
    • Rope hammer curls
    • Face pulls
    • Incline dumbbell curls
  3. Legs (Quads, Glutes, Hamstrings)
    • Barbell squats
    • Leg extentions
    • Hip thrusts
    • Bulgarian split squats
    • RDLs
    • Hamstring curls
